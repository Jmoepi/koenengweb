# **App Name**: Koeneng Hub

## Core Features:

- Information display: Display business information like about us, services and contact details.
- Team Presentation: Visually showcase team members and their roles within Koeneng Hub.
- Contact form: Interactive section for visitors to easily submit inquiries.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep forest green (#228B22) to reflect growth, nature, and the 'tree' concept from the logo idea.
- Background color: Light beige (#F5F5DC), almost white to provide a neutral and clean backdrop.
- Accent color: Electric lime (#32CD32) to represent the tech-driven aspect and add a youthful, vibrant touch, drawing analogy from circuit board colors and youthfulness
- Headline Font: 'Poppins' (sans-serif) for a geometric, contemporary feel that reflects the tech-driven aspect.
- Body Font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for readability and a modern aesthetic, ensuring content is easily accessible.
- Use circuit-inspired icons for tech elements, and organic, growth-related icons where appropriate.  The logo of a tree formed from circuits should be used judiciously.
- A clean, modern layout with a clear visual hierarchy. Utilize whitespace effectively to prevent the design from appearing cluttered.
